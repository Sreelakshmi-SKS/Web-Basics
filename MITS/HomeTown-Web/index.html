Discover the Beauty of Perumbavoor, Kerala

Iringole Kavu

Iringole Kavu is a serene and historic temple located in Perumbavoor, nestled in a sacred grove. It offers visitors a spiritual experience with its lush greenery and peaceful ambiance.

Kalady

Kalady, located near Perumbavoor, is the birthplace of Adi Shankaracharya, one of India's greatest philosophers. The place is rich in history and offers a glimpse into India's cultural heritage.
